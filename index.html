Rules & Regulations

Tournament Terms

🔒

Terms Are Final

No amendments will be made on the day of the tournament. The organisers' published terms and conditions are final and binding for all registered participants without exception.

01 🎱

Rules of Play

Black ball rules apply throughout. Re-entries are permitted in the first round only. To qualify for a re-entry, a first-round loser must be drawn exclusively against another first-round loser. Under no circumstances may a first-round loser face a standby player or any participant who has not yet played a single match in the first round.

📄 Download SA Black Ball Rules
02 🏆

Race Format

All group matches: Race to 7. Semi-finals: Race to 9. Grand Final: Race to 11.

03 🕐

Schedule

Official draw commences at 11:00 AM at Pondi Tavern. All matches begin at 17:00 PM on Saturday 20 June 2026.

04 ⚖️

Officiating

All matches will be officially refereed. The referee's decision is final — no disputes or appeals will be entertained under any circumstances.

05 ⏰

Punctuality

Players arriving after 11:00 AM will be subject to an additional R50 late penalty.

06 💰

Payment

Entry fee: R250. Non-refundable deposit: R150. Outstanding balance of R100 is due on tournament day. The deposit must be paid in full to secure a place.

07 🎯

Draw & Fixtures

Official draw held at 11:00 AM at Pondi Tavern. Once completed, all fixtures will be published on this digital platform for every player to view.

08 ✅

Eligibility

Open to local branch players only — by invitation. Any player omitted due to an administrative oversight will be verified and accommodated under these same terms.

09 ⚡

Level Playing Field

This tournament is conducted on a strictly size-to-size basis — pro vs pro. The draw is structured to ensure each player is matched according to their calibre, upholding the integrity of the Pro 32 Winter Tour.
