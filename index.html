🔒

Imigomo Isemthethweni

Akukho izinguquko ezizovunyelwa ngosuku lomklomelo. Imigomo nemibandela eshicilelwe yabakhiqizi isemthethweni futhi ibophezelekile kubo bonke ababhalisiwe.

01🎱

Imithetho of Play

Imithetho yebhola elimnyama isebenza yonke indawo kuwo wonke umklomelo. Ukuphinda ubhalise kuvunyelwe umzuliswano wokuqala kuphela. Ukuze ufinyelele kuleli lungelo, umdlali owashiswa umzuliswano wokuqala kufanele abhekane nomunye ohluliwe womzuliswano wokuqala kuphela. Ngaphansi kwanoma imuphi umkhawulo, ohluliwe womzuliswano wokuqala akavunyelwe ukuhosha ngokumelana nomuntu olinde noma noma yimuphi umuntu ongakadlali umdlalo owodwa umzuliswano wokuqala. Akukho ukuphinda ubhalise okuvunyelwe kusukela umzuliswano wesibili kuya phambili.

📄 View Official SA Black Ball Imithetho
02🏆

Indlela Yokudlala

All group matches: Race to 7. Semi-finals: Race to 9. The Grand Final: Race to 11.

03🕐

Izikhathi

Ukuhosha okusemthethweni kuqala ngo-11:00 ekuseni ePondi Tavern. Yonke imidlalo yomklomelo iqala ngo-17:00 ntambama ngoMgqibelo, ziyi-20 kuJuni 2026.

04⚖️

Ukwahlulelwa

All matches will be officially refereed. The referee's decision is final and absolute — no disputes, protests, or appeals will be entertained under any circumstances.

05⏰

Ukweba Isikhathi

Abadlali abafika ngemva kwe-11:00 ekuseni bazohlawuliswa inhlawulo engeziwe yama-R50 ngokulibala.

06💰

Inkokhelo Structure

Inkokhelo yokuqala ephelele: R250. Ibhasi elingabuyiswayo lama-R150 liyadingeka ukuze uqinisekise indawo yakho. Insalela yama-R100 ikhokhelwa ngosuku lomncintiswano. Akukho bhasi, akukho ngena.

07🎯

Ukuhosha Nezichibiyelo

The official draw is held at 11:00 AM at Pondi Tavern. Upon completion, all fixtures will be published and displayed on this digital platform for all players to access.

08✅

Ukufanela

Lo mklomelo uvuleleke kubo bonke abadlali. Wonke umuntu wamukelekile ukuzibandakanya ngaphandle kweqembu noma isigodi. Noma yimuphi umdlali owashiywa ohlelweni olwesemthethweni ngenxa yepelapela lokulawulwa uzobe ehlolisiwe futhi etholelwa indawo ngaphansi kwalezi zimigomo nezimibandela.

09⚡

Ubulungiswa Emdlalweni

Lo mncintiswano uqhutshelwa ngokuphelele ngobukhulu nobukhulu — uchwepheshe emelana nochwepheshe. Ukuhosha kuhlongozwa ukuze wonke umudlali ahlangane nomunye ngokwezinga lakhe, ukugcina ubumsulwa bomklomelo nodumo lwe-Pro 32 Umncintiswano Wasebusika.
