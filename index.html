Rules & Regulations

Tournament Terms

🔒

Terms Are Final

No amendments will be entertained on the day of the tournament. The organisers' published terms and conditions are final and binding for all registered participants.

01🎱

Rules of Play

Black ball rules apply exclusively throughout the tournament. Re-entries are permitted in the first round only. To qualify for a re-entry, a first-round loser must face another first-round loser exclusively. Under no circumstances may a first-round loser be drawn against a standby player or any participant who has not yet played a single match in the first round. No re-entries are permitted from the second round onwards.

📄 View Official SA Black Ball Rules
02🏆

Race Format

All group matches: Race to 7. Semi-finals: Race to 9. The Grand Final: Race to 11.

03🕐

Schedule

The official draw commences at 11:00 AM at Pondi Tavern. All competitive matches begin at 17:00 PM on Saturday, 20 June 2026.

04⚖️

Officiating

All matches will be officially refereed. The referee's decision is final and absolute — no disputes, protests, or appeals will be entertained under any circumstances.

05⏰

Punctuality

Players arriving after 11:00 AM will be subject to an additional R50 late penalty.

06💰

Payment Structure

Total entry fee: R250. A non-refundable deposit of R150 is required to secure your place. The outstanding balance of R100 is due on tournament day. No deposit, no entry.

07🎯

Draw & Fixtures

The official draw is held at 11:00 AM at Pondi Tavern. Upon completion, all fixtures will be published and displayed on this digital platform for all players to access.

08✅

Eligibility

This tournament is open to all players. Everyone is welcome to participate regardless of club or branch affiliation. Any player omitted from the official list due to an administrative oversight will be verified and accommodated subject to these same terms and conditions.

09⚡

Level Playing Field

This tournament is conducted on a strictly size-to-size basis — pro versus pro. The draw is structured to ensure every player is matched according to their calibre, preserving the competitive integrity and prestige of the Pro 32 Winter Tour.
